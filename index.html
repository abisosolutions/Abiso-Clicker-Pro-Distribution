✦ WHAT THE APP ACTUALLY DOES

The Windows Host opens a local WebSocket server on port 8181. It reads your open PowerPoint file and sends slide images to the phone over your local WiFi. Nothing leaves your network. No cloud servers are involved in the presentation control.


The Android app connects to that local server, displays slide previews, and sends navigation commands back. It uses Firebase only to verify your license — not to access your files.

✦ WHY WINDOWS SHOWS A WARNING

Microsoft Defender SmartScreen blocks any EXE that does not yet have an Extended Validation (EV) code signing certificate. These certificates cost ₱25,000+ per year. We are a small independent team — we have not purchased one yet.


The warning does not mean the file is dangerous. It means the file is new and unsigned. Scan it on VirusTotal to confirm it is clean, then proceed.

✦ WHY ANDROID BLOCKS THE APK

Android blocks APK files from outside the Play Store by default as a safety measure. This is expected behavior for any sideloaded app — including major apps like Telegram and Grab, which also distribute APKs directly.


We are not on the Play Store yet because the $25 developer fee is outside our current budget. We plan to list the app once we have the funds.

✦ HOW TO VERIFY THE FILE HASH (Advanced)

If you want to verify that the file you downloaded has not been tampered with, compare its SHA256 hash to the official hash listed above the download buttons. Run this command in PowerShell (Windows) or Terminal (Mac/Linux):

PowerShell Get-FileHash "C:\Downloads\Abiso_Clicker_Pro_Host_Setup.exe" -Algorithm SHA256 | Select-Object Hash
Terminal shasum -a 256 ~/Downloads/Abiso_Clicker_Pro_v1.apk

If the output matches the hash on this page exactly, the file is identical to what we released. If it differs by even one character, do not install it.

INSTALLATION GUIDE

Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these instructions exactly. The security warnings are expected and safe to bypass after you have verified the files.

1
Download the Windows Host
Click the "Download for Windows" button above. Your browser may warn that the file could be harmful. Click Keep or Keep anyway to save the file. This is a standard browser caution for any unsigned EXE.
💡 Before proceeding, upload the downloaded EXE to virustotal.com and confirm it gets a clean result. This takes about 60 seconds.
2
Run the Installer — Bypass SmartScreen
Double-click the downloaded EXE. Windows Defender SmartScreen will appear with the message "Windows protected your PC". This is expected for any new unsigned software.
⚠️ Do not close this window. Click "More info" (the small text link below the warning message). A new button labeled "Run anyway" will appear at the bottom. Click it to proceed.
3
Complete the Installation
The standard Windows installer will open. Follow the on-screen steps — click Next, accept the license agreement, and click Install. The program will be added to your Start Menu.
💡 If Windows Firewall asks to allow network access for AbisoPro.exe, click Allow access. This is required for your phone to connect to the laptop.
4
Run AbisoPro as Administrator (Recommended)
Right-click the Abiso Clicker Pro shortcut in your Start Menu and select Run as administrator. This gives the Host the permissions needed to control PowerPoint smoothly, especially for media-heavy presentations.
5
Open PowerPoint and Note the IP Address
Launch Microsoft PowerPoint and open your presentation file. The Abiso Host window will display your laptop's local IP address (it looks like 192.168.1.X). Write this down — you will enter it in the phone app.
6
Keep the Host Window Open
Do not close the Abiso Host window during your presentation session. Keep it running in the background. You can minimize it, but closing it will disconnect your phone.
1
Download the APK to Your Phone
Open this page on your Android phone's browser and tap "Download APK". Your browser may show a warning that this file type could be harmful. Tap Download anyway or OK to save the file.
💡 You can also download it to your PC and transfer it to your phone via USB or Google Drive.
2
Allow Installation from Unknown Sources
Tap the downloaded APK file (usually in your Downloads folder or notification bar). Android will show a message: "For your security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source."
⚠️ Tap Settings in that prompt. A toggle labeled "Allow from this source" or "Install unknown apps" will appear. Enable it. Then press the back button — the install dialog will resume automatically.
3
Review Permissions and Install
Android will show the app's permissions before installing. You will see only Internet and Camera (for QR scanning). No access to your contacts, storage, or location. Tap Install to proceed.
4
Play Protect Warning (if shown)
Google Play Protect may show a warning that the app was not verified by Play Protect. This is standard for any sideloaded APK. Tap "Install anyway" to continue. This warning appears for all apps installed outside the Play Store.
💡 If you want maximum assurance before this step, scan the APK using your phone's built-in antivirus or a free app like Malwarebytes Mobile before installing.
5
Connect and Start Presenting
Open Abiso Clicker Pro on your phone. Make sure your phone and laptop are on the same WiFi network. Enter the IP address shown in the Host window, or tap the QR icon to scan the QR code displayed by the Host. Tap CONNECT.
6
Disable "Unknown Sources" After Install (Recommended)
For best security practice, go back to Settings → Apps → Special App Access → Install unknown apps and disable the permission you granted in Step 2. The app is already installed — you no longer need this permission enabled.
COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common concerns from first-time users.

Is this a virus? My antivirus flagged it. +
No. Both files are clean. Antivirus programs sometimes flag unsigned software with what is called a "false positive" — this means the program does not recognize the file (because it is new and unsigned), not that it found actual malicious code. To verify this yourself, upload the file to VirusTotal.com. VirusTotal checks the file against 70+ antivirus engines. A clean result from that scan is far more reliable than a single antivirus program flagging a new file.
Why isn't the app on the Google Play Store? +
Publishing on the Google Play Store requires a one-time $25 USD developer registration fee. As a small independent team based in Davao City, Philippines, we are working toward this. We plan to publish on the Play Store as soon as we have the budget — which your purchase directly helps fund. In the meantime, the APK is distributed directly from our official GitHub releases page.
Does the app access my PowerPoint files or send them to the internet? +
No. Your files never leave your local network. The Windows Host reads your open PowerPoint file and converts each slide to an image. That image is sent directly to your phone over your local WiFi — never to any cloud server. The Android app connects to your laptop directly. The only internet traffic is the license verification with Firebase when you first sign in.
My phone and laptop can't connect. What do I do? +
The most common cause is that your phone and laptop are on different networks. Both must be on the same WiFi network. If your office WiFi blocks device-to-device connections (common in corporate and school networks), use the built-in Mobile Hotspot on your Windows laptop and connect your phone's WiFi to it. This creates a private, direct link between the two devices that bypasses network restrictions.
What happens to my Pro license if I lose my phone? +
Your license is tied to your Google account, not your physical device. If you get a new phone, simply download the APK, open the app, and sign in with the same Google account. Your Pro status will be restored automatically — no support ticket, no extra payment.
Does it work without internet during my presentation? +
Yes. Once you have signed in at least once with internet, your Pro license is cached to your phone's local storage. After that, the app does not need internet to confirm your license. The presentation control itself runs entirely over local WiFi between your phone and laptop — it does not require internet access at all.
What are the system requirements? +
Windows Host: Windows 10 or later, Microsoft PowerPoint (any recent version), .NET 8 Runtime (the installer will prompt if not present). At least 4 GB RAM recommended for smooth slide rendering.

Android App: Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later. Any Android phone made after 2018 will work. Both devices must be on the same local network.

Abiso Clicker Pro is built by a small independent software team based in Davao City, Philippines. We believe the best way to earn trust is through transparency — which is why we publish file hashes, explain exactly what the app does and does not do, and encourage you to scan the files yourself before installing.
